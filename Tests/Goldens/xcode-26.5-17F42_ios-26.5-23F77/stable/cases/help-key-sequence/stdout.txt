OVERVIEW: Press a sequence of keys by their keycodes on the simulator.

Press multiple keys in sequence using their HID keycode values.
Each key will be pressed and released before the next key is pressed.

Examples:
  axe key-sequence 11,8,15,15,18 --udid SIMULATOR_UDID    # Type "hello" (h=11,
e=8, l=15, l=15, o=18)
  axe key-sequence 40,40,40 --udid SIMULATOR_UDID          # Press Enter 3
times
  axe key-sequence 224,4,225 --udid SIMULATOR_UDID        # Ctrl+A (Ctrl=224,
A=4, release Ctrl=225)

USAGE: axe key-sequence --keycodes <keycodes> [--delay <delay>] --udid <udid>

OPTIONS:
  --keycodes <keycodes>   Comma-separated list of HID keycodes to press in
                          sequence.
  --delay <delay>         Delay between key presses in seconds (default: 0.1).
  --udid <udid>           The UDID of the simulator.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
