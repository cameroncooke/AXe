OVERVIEW: Press a hardware button on the simulator.

Available buttons: apple-pay, home, lock, side-button, siri

Examples:
  axe button home --udid SIMULATOR_UDID
  axe button lock --duration 2.0 --udid SIMULATOR_UDID
  axe button siri --udid SIMULATOR_UDID

USAGE: axe button <button-type> [--duration <duration>] --udid <udid>

ARGUMENTS:
  <button-type>           The button to press. (values: apple-pay, home, lock,
                          side-button, siri)

OPTIONS:
  --duration <duration>   Duration to hold the button in seconds (optional).
  --udid <udid>           The UDID of the simulator.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
