OVERVIEW: Perform a low-level point-to-point drag using explicit touch move
events.

USAGE: axe drag --start-x <start-x> --start-y <start-y> --end-x <end-x> --end-y <end-y> [--duration <duration>] [--steps <steps>] [--pre-delay <pre-delay>] [--post-delay <post-delay>] --udid <udid>

OPTIONS:
  --start-x <start-x>     The X coordinate of the starting point.
  --start-y <start-y>     The Y coordinate of the starting point.
  --end-x <end-x>         The X coordinate of the ending point.
  --end-y <end-y>         The Y coordinate of the ending point.
  --duration <duration>   Duration of the drag movement in seconds. (default:
                          0.6)
  --steps <steps>         Number of touch move events to emit during the drag.
                          (default: 60)
  --pre-delay <pre-delay> Delay before starting the drag in seconds.
  --post-delay <post-delay>
                          Delay after completing the drag in seconds.
  --udid <udid>           The UDID of the simulator.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
